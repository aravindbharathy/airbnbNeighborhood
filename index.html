Airbnb Neighborhoods

Our goal is to help Airbnb users identify safe and convenient neighborhoods in Chicago to narrow their search on Airbnb. The map below shows the relative safety and Convenience of each neighborhood. The darker the color of the neighborhoods the higher the score. Hover or Click on a neighborhood to see the details of the scoring and average prices by room type for that neighborhood. Click to zoom in on the neighborhood to see the best locations within the neighborhood.

The overview map combines the safety score and the WalkScore of each neighborhood. Our formula weights safety at 70% and WalkScore at 30%. Our safety measure calculates the number of homicides per 100,000 people divided by the number of overall crimes per 100,000 people in each neighborhood. The WalkScore is provided by www.walkscore.com. The right sidebar shows how the selected neighborhood compares to the city average in safety and convenience.
